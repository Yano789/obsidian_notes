A clique in a simple [[Undirected Graph]] 𝐺 is a subset of vertices that are pairwise adjacent, i.e. **any two distinct vertices are adjacent.**

For integers 𝑘 ≥ 0, a 𝒌-clique is a clique of size 𝑘.
